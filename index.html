About Me

I am a Power Analyst with a background in Electrical & Computer Engineering, specialised in Embedded Generation and Demand-Side Management.

Throughout my career, I had the opportunity to join various innovative projects in the energy sector while working for an Electricity & Gas Supplier, a Distribution Network Operator, an Energy Aggregator and a Research Institute among others, which made me capable of understanding and developing solutions for a wide range of challenges in the energy industry.

My current interest is around optimisation & forecasting methods for short- & long-term planning of flexible energy assets in electricity markets, as well as ways of monitoring & managing the associated risks.
